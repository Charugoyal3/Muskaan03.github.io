How to make homemade pizza

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup butter, softened
  2. 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 2 tsp. vanilla 2-1/4 cups flour 1 tsp. baking soda 1 pkg. (300 g) Baker's Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

    In large bowl, mix first 4 ingredients. Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes. Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes. Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan. Pat into a circle.
