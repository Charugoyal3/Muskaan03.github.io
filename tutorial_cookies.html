How to make homemade pizza

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup butter, softened
  2. 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 2 tsp. vanilla
  6. 2-1/4 cups flour
  7. 1 tsp. baking soda
  8. 1 pkg. (300 g) Baker's Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

    Heat oven to 375Â°F. Beat butter, sugars, eggs and vanilla in large bowl until light and fluffy. Stir in flour and baking soda until well blended. Add chocolate chips; mix well. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough, 2 inches apart, onto baking sheets. Bake 10 to 12 min. or until lightly browned. Cool on baking sheet 3 min. Remove to wire racks; cool completely.
